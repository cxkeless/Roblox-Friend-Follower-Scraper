## How to Use

1. Open Roblox in your browser and log in to your account
2. Open the browser developer console (F12 or right-click > Inspect > Console)
3. Copy and paste the entire contents of `robloxFollowerIdsScraper.js` into the console
4. Press Enter to run the script
5. When prompted, enter the Roblox username or userId of the player you want to scrape
6. Choose whether to scrape "followers" or "friends" (defaults to followers if left blank)
7. Wait for the script to finish scraping
8. The user IDs will be automatically copied to your clipboard

## Notes

- You must be logged into Roblox for the script to work
- The script includes a 250ms delay between requests to avoid rate limiting
- Results are displayed in the console and copied to your clipboard
